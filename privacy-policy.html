WallGen+ App Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2025

1. Introduction

WallGen+ ("I", “we”, “our”, or “the app”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our app (WallGen+).

2. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Data:

  • Device information such as OS version and/or Device ID for app (WallGen+) functionality.

User-Provided Data:

  • Email address only if you choose to send feedback or report bugs.
  • No personal accounts or login information are required.

Wallpaper Images:

  • All images are generated through AI model.
  • No images are stored on our servers; they are stored/cached directly on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display wallpapers and ensure smooth app (WallGen+) operation.
  • To respond to your feedback or bug reports.
  • To improve app (WallGen+) functionality and provide updates.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.
  • Images generated through AI model are not owned by the app or its developer(s).

5. Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Your Choices

  • You can disable daily wallpapers at any time in the app (WallGen+) settings.
  • You may choose not to provide email feedback.
  • You may choose not to generate images through AI model or the AI feature provided in the app (WallGen+).
  • You may choose to generate more AI images beyond free limit by purchasing appropriate upgrade plans in the app.
  • You may choose to generate limited number of AI-powered images in a day by watching ads.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updated privacy policy will be published in the app (WallGen+) and on this page.
